How It Works

Our AI-powered system analyzes the job description to identify key requirements and client pain points. It then matches these with your skills and experience to create a compelling, personalized proposal that addresses the client's specific needs.

  • Skill Extraction: We identify the skills and expertise required for the job.
  • Pain Point Analysis: We determine the client's challenges and motivations.
  • Experience Matching: We highlight your relevant experience that addresses the client's needs.
  • Proposal Generation: We create a compelling headline and proposal text tailored to the specific job.
